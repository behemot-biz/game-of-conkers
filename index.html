Welcome to Conker Champions!

Dive into the traditional playground duel of conkers! In this digital twist on a classic game, you face off against the computer in a battle of both luck and strategy. Attack with your conker, armed with unique strength and durability, and prepare for a clash that will test your nerve and skill.

How It Works:

  • Each player takes a turn to swing their conker at their opponentâ€™s.
  • The impact reduces the opponent's conker durability based on your conker's strength.
  • The game continues in turns until one conker breaks.

Goal:

Break your opponent's conker before yours falls apart to claim the title of Conker Champion!
